Is Jacob Sitting on His Butt?
Yes.

What the hell is this?

It's a website which tells the world when I'm sitting on my butt making games (Paradigm, a point and click adventure game) via a pressure mat on my chair. Unfortunately not via magic, that would be cooler.

COULDN'T YOU JUST LEAVE A CHRISTMAS HAM ON YOUR CHAIR?

Yes. BUT, I've also set up a time lapse camera which is also triggered by the pressure mat. I compile all the recordings each week and post it on youtube here. Sorry that I'm not the one to fulfill your Christmas ham fantasies.

Why, Just whyyy?$?

I'm working on my own to make an adventure game which I Kickstarted. I'm always trying to find new fun ways to motivate myself and avoid looking at dumb pictures on the internet. This way being recorded I have no choice but to work and not procrastinate or I'll look dumb.
